Siftulator Cheat Sheet

Siftulator is the software simulator that helps test apps for Sifteo cubes.

Siftulator In Action

Controlling Cubes

Click and drag to move a block.

Hold shift and drag to tilt a block.

Right click to press the button.

Press the Z key to rotate 90 degrees counterclockwise.

Press the X key to rotate 90 degrees clockwise.

Press the C key to flip.

Hold the A key to shake [there is currently no visual feedback; it’s a to-do]

Toolbar controls

  • Log: Opens a dialog that displays the stream of messages between runner and simulator.
  • Number of Siftables: Enter a number or click on the up/down arrows to add/remove extra cubes.
  • Scramble:
  • Straighten:
  • Assets: Click on "No assets" and pick a directory to load the images in that directory. Only \*.siftimg files are read.
  • Firmware version: The 0.0.0 button is used to spoof a firmware version. Set this if the runner complains about your simulated cubes needing a firmware update.

ImageHelper Command Line

-f: will make the simulator capture frames for every repaint issued. Keep running with this on and you’ll fill up your hard drive!
