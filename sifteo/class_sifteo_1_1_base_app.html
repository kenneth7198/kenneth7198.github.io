bool Sifteo.BaseApp.IsIdle [get, set]

Check whether the connection to the Cubes is idle or not. It's generally recommended to only send more commands to the cubes when the system is idle, to prevent queueing up more commands than they can execute at once. The IdleEvent is also available to be notified when the system transitions to idle.
